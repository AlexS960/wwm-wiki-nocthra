## How to Befriend Bodhi

### Tell that You Love Cats

Bodhi loves cats, and so use this information in order for him to have a good impression of you. Tell him that you love cats and know a few tricks that you use to bond with them. This will give Bodhi a good impression of you, making it easier to befriend you.

### Example of Successful Conversation

| (sitting quietly beneath the ancient tree, six cats weaving around his robes) Amitabha ... Wanderer, you have come.                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **I see you are also a fellow cat lover, friend.**                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| (smiling gently, stroking one of the cats) Indeed, these little ones bring much peace and companionship. They remind me that even in solitude, there is connection. What brings you to this quiet place?                                                                       |
| **I heard from one of travelers I met throughout my journey that a monk lives here who loves his feline companions.**                                                                                                                                                          |
| (nods thoughtfully, eyes reflecting the serenity of the surroundings) Many travelers speak of this place, yet few find their way here. It seems fate has brought you to me. What do you seek in these tranquil lands?                                                          |
| **To show you about my cat call that makes them dance**                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| (raises an eyebrow, intrigued but maintaining a calm demeanor) A cat call, you say? That sounds quite intriguing. Please, demonstrate if you would.                                                                                                                            |
| **(you whispered in a tone only cats can hear ... and one of them danced to the beat)**                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| (eyes widen slightly, a gentle smile forming) Remarkable! Your bond with these creatures is strong indeed. Such harmony between man and nature is rare. Tell me, what led you to develop such a skill?                                                                         |
| **In order to show you that there are people who also loves cats as you do, friend.**                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| (nods appreciatively, stroking another cat) It is heartening to meet someone who shares this love. Cats teach us much about grace and contentment. In what ways has your journey taught you about loneliness and companionship?                                                |
| **it taught me that isolation can also be a form of friendship, as it gives you peace with yourself.**                                                                                                                                                                         |
| (smiles softly, nodding) Wise words, indeed. Isolation can be a path to inner peace and self-discovery. The cats and I understand this well. Do you find solace in your own company as well?                                                                                   |
| **but of course. (pets cat)**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| (gently nods, watching the cat being petted) It is good to see such comfort in solitude. The cats and I often reflect on the nature of companionship. Sometimes, the most profound connections are those we form within ourselves. What else have you learned on your travels? |
| **(you showed a magic trick and a cat appears out of thin air) That you can also make magic tricks with your fellow feline companions.**                                                                                                                                       |
| (palms pressed together, eyes glowing with serene wisdom) Thank you, Wanderer, for sharing the truths of life with me. At last, our hearts are in harmony.Bodhi expresses gratitude and begins to ponder deeply.(Affection +100, becomes Revered)                              |

Note that AI conversations can be unpredictable and even with the same inputs, the same result is not necessarily guaranteed.

## How to Succeed in AI Chat

### Reset Dialogue When Stuck

If your conversation has gone off the rails, or doesn't end no matter what you say, it's best to simply **reset and start anew** from a clean slate by clicking the button with a Refresh icon at the top of the chat box. Instead of trying several methods within one conversation, trying one strategy at a time and refreshing if it doesn't go anywhere is generally more effective.

### Return to Login Screen if Combat Begins

Sometimes, NPCs will suddenly become violent during AI dialogues. When this happens, it's best to just bail out to the login screen; when you return, the NPC will be found just as they were before the conversation started.

If you accidentally kill a dialogue-capable NPC, our experience has shown that they will respawn, but this has not been heavily tested, so it's best to simply avoid engaging them in combat.

### Double-Check the Hint

Even if the NPC appears to be happy with the result of your discussion, this won't always end the dialogue. Double-checking the text written in the hint will imply the win condition, so try strategies which will lead the dialogue towards that result.
