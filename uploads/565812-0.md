## How to Succeed in AI Chatbot

### Reset Dialogue When Stuck

If your conversation has gone off the rails, or doesn't end no matter what you say, it's best to simply **reset and start anew** the AI Chatbot from a clean slate by clicking the button with a Refresh icon at the top of the chat box. Instead of trying several methods within one conversation, trying one strategy at a time and refreshing if it doesn't go anywhere is generally more effective.

### Return to Login Screen if Combat Begins

Sometimes, NPCs will suddenly become violent during AI Chatbot dialogues. When this happens, it's best to just bail out to the login screen; when you return, the NPC will be found just as they were before the conversation started.

If you accidentally kill a dialogue-capable NPC, our experience has shown that they will respawn, but this has not been heavily tested, so it's best to simply avoid engaging them in combat.

### Double-Check the Hint

Even if the NPC appears to be happy with the result of your discussion, this won't always end the dialogue. Double-checking the text written in the hint will imply the win condition, so try strategies which will lead the dialogue towards that result.

## What are NPCs in Where Winds Meet?

### NPCs Have AI Integrated Conversations

Aside from regular NPCs with static dialogue, there will be some NPCs that are integrated with AI generated responses which means that you can **chat with them** and have **dynamic responses as a reply** to your messages.

### Befriend NPCs to Gain Rewards

Sending NPCs the right responses to their dialogue will raise your relationship rank level with them. At certain relationship thresholds, NPCs might even give you rewards such as **gifts**.
