## How to Solve Riddles

| [NPC Provides a Clue Hint](#hm%5F100) [Answer Within the Allotted Turn Attempts](#hm%5F101) [Guess the Correct Word](#hm%5F102) |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

### NPC Provides a Clue Hint 

The Riddles NPC will give you a one or two-worded clue hint to help you start guessing the answer.

### Answer Within the Allotted Turn Attempts 

Riddles lets you guess a set amount of times before you lose. There is also a timer at the top of your screen that skips a turn if you fail to guess within the time limit.

The amount for turn attempts for Riddles may differ depending on the Intelligence requirement.

### Guess the Correct Word 

The NPC AI chatbot will give you a prompt and you have to figure out the answer by typing out guesses or asking a question to the NPC AI chatbot.

## Riddle Tips and Tricks

### Exchange Bells for Commerce Coins

Attempting to solve riddles burns Commerce Coins quicker than you think. To quickly restock on Commerce Coins, simply complete any 2 Meow Meow challenges for the Bell rewards, then go to **Meow Meow Temple** on Halo Peak. Exchange 10 Bells for 10 Small Boxes of Commerce Coins containing 2500 coins per box, for a total of **25000 Commerce Coins**!
